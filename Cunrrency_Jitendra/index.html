Currency

Foreign exchange is the most liquid, decentralised, over-the-counter market, with a daily volume of more than $6.6 trillion in 2019. Individuals and institutions from all over the world buy and sell currencies on the currency market. The foreign exchange market is crucial because it allows currency conversion, which enables international trade and investment. Participants include banks, central banks, investment management firms, and investors.

Why Currency

Most when people think about trading, the first and final thing that comes to mind is stock trading. On the other hand, Currency trading like stock trading also takes place through an exchange. Mostly currencies are traded by importers and exporters whose revenues are determined by the exchange rate. You may profit by buying and selling currencies. It is the most liquid market in the world since it trades in large quantities on a daily basis. The strength of a country's currency trading is determined by demand-supply, geopolitical situation, deficits, import-export, and economic health.
